Hi, there! I'm Rita. Nice to finally meet you.

I'm originally from Cleveland, Ohio and have recently moved to the Detroit area. Althogh I'm fiercely proud of where I've come from, I'm happy to be in a vibrant city with a rich history and interesting people. There's always so much to do here!

I graduated from The Ohio State University in 2014 with a B.A. in English and a Minor in Professional Writing. During the course of my college years, I held internships at places like Columbus Museum of Art, a startup called Xcelerate Media, and Department of Human Resources and Planning at the University. When I graduated, I worked for the Sherwin-Williams corporate office as a Marketing Co-Op and also as a E-Commerce Financial Processor. My most recent experience is working as a Sales Consultant at Progressive Insurance, where I met and exceeded my sales goals each week, having one of the highest close rates on my team despite being the newest memeber.

Although I loved the challenge of sales, I am looking to transition to use my skills helping others in a different capacity: front-end development. I'd like to help make interfaces more user-friendly so that people can access the information that they need in a way that feels intuitive. My goal is to create code that looks and feels effortless to the end user.
